Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduConnect?

EduConnect is a platform that connects qualified educators with government schools to provide expertise and opportunities for growth.

How can I register as an educator?

You can register as an educator by visiting our website and clicking on the "Register Now" button. Fill in the required information and follow the instructions.

How do I post teaching vacancies?

Posting teaching vacancies is easy. Simply log in to your EduConnect account, navigate to the dashboard, and follow the steps to create and manage vacancies.
